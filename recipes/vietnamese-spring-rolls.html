Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Home

Description

These spring rolls are delicious and fresh. They can be served as lunch or dinner. A peanut sauce recipe is disclosed, but they can easily be paired with other sauces.

Ingredients

Spring rolls:

  • Rice paper
  • Rice noodles
  • Tofu | Shrimp | Egg (choose one or more)
  • Carrot, julienned
  • Cucumber, cut into strips
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Paprika, cut into strips
  • Fresh mint
  • Fresh coriander

Sauce:

  • Peanut sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar or honey
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Siracha

Steps

Tofu:
  1. Press water from the tofu beforehand. Cut it into strips and roll in cornstarch.
  2. Bake the tofu in sesame oil.
  3. Optional: Add some of the peanut sauce in at the end and fry until sauce becomes a glaze.

The egg can be stir-fried and the shrimp just cooked in oil for 2 minutes. Other than that it's basically just assembly.
