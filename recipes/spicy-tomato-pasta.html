Spicy Tomato Pasta

Home

Description

A simple tomato pasta recipe adapted from this Uit Paulines Keuken recipe. It makes around 3 portions. It can be made in one pot.

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta
  • 500g tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 1 red (pointed) paprika
  • 4 serrano peppers (rehydrate an hour beforehand if using dried)
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 400ml chicken bouillon
  • 1 rosemary twig
  • 1tl oregano
  • siracha sauce
  • hand of basil
  • parmesan cheese (or other)
  • salt and pepper
Optional: Add vegan meatballs

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic, pepper and onion.
  2. Take a large pot (may be shallow) and fry the above on medium heat.
  3. Wash the tomatoes and paprika, chop them, and add them.
  4. Optioinal: Cook for a while, then blend using a stick blender.
  5. Add the chicken bouillon, pasta and rosemary. With the lid on, boil for duration on the pasta packaging. Stir occasionally.
  6. Remove the rosemary.
  7. Plate and add shredded cheese.
